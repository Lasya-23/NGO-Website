Our Mission

Basthi ki pathshala is a grassroots initiative dedicated to bringing education to every child in marginalized communities. We believe learning should be accessible, joyful, and inclusive.

Our Impact

Over 1,000 children reached through weekend schools, volunteer-run classes, and skill-based workshops. Every hour you give makes a lifetime of difference for a child.
